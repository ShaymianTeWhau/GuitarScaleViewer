# **Privacy Policy**

**Effective Date:** November 6, 2025

## **Overview**

Guitar Scale Viewer ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

## **Information Collection and Use**

The App **does not collect, store, or share any personal information**.

* No user accounts or personal identifiers are used.
* The App does not access your contacts, files, or location.
* The App does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools.

## **Third-Party Services**

The App **does not connect to or use any third-party services**.
All functionality is performed entirely on your device, without internet communication.

## **Data Storage**

All data displayed or generated by the App (such as selected scales or instrument tunings) exists **only temporarily on your device’s memory**. It is not transmitted or saved externally.

## **Children’s Privacy**

The App is safe for all ages. Since no personal data is collected, the App fully complies with children’s online privacy protection standards.

## **Changes to This Policy**

If future versions of the App introduce new features that affect privacy, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. You will be able to view the latest version at any time from within the App or its official distribution page.
